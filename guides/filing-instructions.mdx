---
title: Filing Instructions
description: Complete step-by-step guide to filing at Free File Fillable Forms
---

EZFile calculates your tax return, but you must file it yourself at [Free File Fillable Forms](https://www.freefilefillableforms.com/). This guide walks you through every field.

<Note>
Free File Fillable Forms does very little math for you. You must calculate most values yourself and enter them manually -- that's exactly what EZFile does.
</Note>

## Before You Start

You'll need:

- Your completed return summary from EZFile
- Your original W-2 (for your full SSN)
- Your bank account info (for direct deposit)
- Prior year AGI or self-select PIN

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Free File Fillable Forms">
    Visit https://www.freefilefillableforms.com/
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create an account">
    You'll need:
    - Your full SSN
    - Date of birth
    - Prior year AGI (from last year's return) OR prior year self-select PIN
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Form 1040">
    Choose "Form 1040" (or "Form 1040-SR" if you're 65 or older)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Personal Information

Click on each field and type the value:

| Field | What to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|
| First name and middle initial | Your legal name | W-2 Box e |
| Last name | Your legal last name | W-2 Box e |
| Social security number | Your **full** SSN | W-2 Box a |
| Filing status | Check "Single" | Always Single for EZFile users |
| Digital assets question | Check "No" | Unless you had crypto |

<Warning>
**Important:** Unlike EZFile, you WILL enter your full SSN on the actual tax form. EZFile masks it for privacy during calculation, but you need the real number to file.
</Warning>

## Step 3: Address Section

| Field | What to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Home address | Your current mailing address | May differ from W-2 Box f |
| City, state, ZIP | Current city, state, ZIP | -- |

## Step 4: Income Section

| Line | Field Name | Value to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1a | Wages, salaries, tips | [From your return] | Sum of all W-2 Box 1 amounts |
| 1z | Add lines 1a through 1h | Same as Line 1a | For W-2 only, just 1a |
| 9 | Total income | Line 1z + Line 8 | Line 8 from Schedule 1 if applicable |

## Step 5: Adjustments Section

<Accordion title="If you have student loan interest">
  You'll need to add Schedule 1 first:
  
  1. Click "Add Form" or "+" button
  2. Search for "Schedule 1"
  3. Select "Schedule 1 (Form 1040)"
  
  Then fill Schedule 1:
  
  | Line | Field Name | Value |
  |---|---|---|
  | 21 | Student loan interest deduction | [From your return] |
  | 26 | Total adjustments | Same as Line 21 |
  
  The value flows to Form 1040 Line 10.
</Accordion>

| Line | Field Name | Value to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 10 | Adjustments to income | [Schedule 1, Line 26] | Student loan interest |
| 11 | Adjusted gross income | Line 9 - Line 10 | This is your AGI |

## Step 6: Deductions Section

| Line | Field Name | Value to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 12a | Standard deduction | $15,750 or $17,750 | $15,750 under 65, $17,750 if 65+ |
| 12b | Charitable deduction | $0 | Not itemizing |
| 12c | Add Schedule 1-A | [From your return] | Tips/overtime/car/senior |
| 13 | Qualified business income | $0 | W-2 only, no QBI |
| 14 | Total deductions | 12a + 12b + 12c + 13 | Sum |
| 15 | Taxable income | Line 11 - Line 14 | Cannot be negative |

<Accordion title="If you have Schedule 1-A deductions">
  You'll need to add Schedule 1-A:
  
  1. Click "Add Form"
  2. Search for "Schedule 1-A"
  3. Select "Schedule 1-A (Form 1040)"
  
  Then fill Schedule 1-A:
  
  | Line | Field Name | Value |
  |---|---|---|
  | 1 | Tip income deduction | [From your return] |
  | 2 | Overtime pay deduction | [From your return] |
  | 3 | Auto loan interest | [From your return] |
  | 4 | Senior deduction | [From your return] |
  | 5 | Total | Sum of Lines 1-4 |
  
  The value flows to Form 1040 Line 12c.
</Accordion>

## Step 7: Tax Section

| Line | Field Name | Value to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 16 | Tax | [From your return] | Calculated from brackets |
| 17 | Schedule 2, line 21 | $0 | No AMT |
| 18 | Add lines 16 and 17 | Same as Line 16 | -- |
| 19 | Nonrefundable credits | [From your return] | Saver's Credit if eligible |
| 22 | Subtract Line 19 from 18 | Line 18 - Line 19 | Cannot go below $0 |
| 23 | Other taxes | $0 | -- |
| 24 | Total tax | Line 22 + Line 23 | Your total federal tax |

<Accordion title="If you have Saver's Credit">
  You'll need to add Form 8880:
  
  1. Click "Add Form"
  2. Search for "8880"
  3. Select "Form 8880"
  
  Then fill Form 8880:
  
  | Line | Value |
  |---|---|
  | 1 | Retirement contributions (W-2 Box 12) |
  | 2 | $2,000 |
  | 3 | min(Line 1, Line 2) |
  | 4 | Credit rate (50%, 20%, or 10% based on AGI) |
  | 5 | Line 3 × Line 4 |
  
  The value flows to Schedule 3 Line 4, then to Form 1040 Line 19.
</Accordion>

## Step 8: Payments Section

| Line | Field Name | Value to Enter | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| 25a | Federal tax withheld from W-2s | [Sum of all W-2 Box 2] | From your return |
| 25d | Total federal tax withheld | Same as Line 25a | -- |
| 27a | Earned income credit | [From your return] | If eligible |
| 33 | Total payments and credits | Line 25d + Line 27a | Total |

## Step 9: Refund or Amount Owed

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="If you're getting a refund">
    | Line | Field Name | Value |
    |---|---|---|
    | 34 | Overpaid | Line 33 - Line 24 |
    | 35a | Refunded to you | Same as Line 34 |
    | 35b | Routing number | Your bank routing number |
    | 35c | Account type | Checking or Savings |
    | 35d | Account number | Your bank account number |
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="If you owe">
    | Line | Field Name | Value |
    |---|---|---|
    | 37 | Amount you owe | Line 24 - Line 33 |
    | 38 | Estimated tax penalty | Usually $0 |
    
    You can pay when filing or set up a payment plan.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 10: Signature Section

| Field | What to Enter |
|---|---|
| Your signature | E-sign with Free File Fillable Forms |
| Date | Date you file |
| Occupation | Your job title |
| Identity Protection PIN | If you received one from IRS (optional) |
| Phone number | Your phone number |

## Step 11: Review and Submit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review all values">
    Double-check every number matches your EZFile return summary
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Run the form check">
    Free File Fillable Forms will flag any obvious errors
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="E-sign and submit">
    Use the e-signature feature to sign and file electronically
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save confirmation">
    You'll receive a confirmation number -- save this and download a PDF copy
  </Step>
</Steps>
