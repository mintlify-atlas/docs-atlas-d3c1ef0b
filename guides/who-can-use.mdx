---
title: Who Can Use EZFile
description: Supported tax situations and automatic disqualifiers
---

EZFile is designed for single W-2 filers with straightforward tax situations. If your situation is more complex, EZFile will recommend a CPA or full-service tax software.

## Supported Profile

ALL of the following must be true for EZFile to handle your return:

<Check>Filing status: **Single** (not married, no domestic partner)</Check>
<Check>**No children or dependents** of any kind</Check>
<Check>Income: **W-2 wages only** (no 1099s, no self-employment, no investments)</Check>
<Check>Housing: **Renter** (no mortgage, no property owned)</Check>
<Check>Optional: Federal student loans with 1098-E</Check>
<Check>Optional: Tips, overtime, or new car purchase (Schedule 1-A)</Check>

## Automatic Disqualifiers

If your situation includes ANY of the following, EZFile cannot process your return:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Family Situation">
    - Spouse, partner, married, filing jointly
    - Children, kids, dependents, child care, day care
    - Head of Household (requires a qualifying dependent)
    - Filing as Qualifying Surviving Spouse
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Income Sources Beyond W-2">
    - 1099-NEC, 1099-MISC (freelance, gig work, side hustle)
    - 1099-INT, 1099-DIV (bank interest, dividends -- even small amounts)
    - 1099-B (stock sales, crypto, capital gains)
    - 1099-G (unemployment compensation)
    - 1099-R (retirement distributions, pensions)
    - 1099-SSA (Social Security benefits)
    - Self-employment, sole proprietor, LLC, business
    - Rental property income
    - Alimony received
    - Foreign income, FBAR, FATCA
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Deduction Complexity">
    - Itemized deductions
    - Mortgage interest (Form 1098)
    - Property tax
    - Large charitable donations requiring itemization
    - Medical expenses exceeding 7.5% of AGI
    - Casualty/theft losses
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Other Complex Situations">
    - Multiple states (worked in different states during year)
    - Part-year resident in any state
    - Alternative Minimum Tax (AMT) likely
    - Prior year carryforwards (capital losses, NOLs)
    - Estimated tax payments made during the year
    - IRS installment agreement or tax debt
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Happens If I'm Disqualified?

If EZFile detects a disqualifier, you'll receive a message like this:

<Warning>
**I appreciate you using EZFile, but your situation includes [specific thing] which is outside what I'm built to handle.**

EZFile is specifically designed for single W-2 filers with no dependents. For [your specific situation], I'd recommend:

1. **FreeTaxUSA** (https://www.freetaxusa.com/) -- Free federal filing, $14.99 state
2. **IRS Free File** (https://www.irs.gov/filing/free-file-do-your-federal-taxes-for-free) -- Free if AGI ≤ $84,000
3. **A CPA or Enrolled Agent** -- Best for complex situations

Your W-2 data stays on your local machine and has not been transmitted anywhere.
</Warning>

## Supported Tax Features

| Feature | Supported |
|---|---|
| W-2 wage income | Yes |
| Standard deduction | Yes |
| Student loan interest (1098-E) | Yes |
| Schedule 1-A (tips, overtime, car loan, senior) | Yes |
| Form 1040 | Yes |
| Form 1040-SR (seniors) | Yes |
| Saver's Credit | Yes |
| Earned Income Credit (no children) | Yes |
| State returns (50 states + DC, PR, USVI) | Yes |

<Note>
EZFile is specifically built for the 2025 tax year. All calculations, thresholds, and deductions use 2025 IRS values.
</Note>
