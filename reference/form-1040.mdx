This reference maps every Form 1040 line number to its purpose, source data, and calculation method for single W-2 filers.

## Personal Information

| Field | Description | Source |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Your first name and middle initial | Your legal first name | W-2 Box e |
| Last name | Your legal last name | W-2 Box e |
| Your social security number | Your full SSN | W-2 Box a (original document) |
| Filing status | Check "Single" | Always Single for EZFile |
| Digital assets question | Check "No" (unless crypto held) | User confirmation |

<Note>
  Unlike EZFile, you WILL enter your full SSN on the actual tax form. EZFile masks it for privacy during calculation, but the IRS requires the real number to file.
</Note>

## Address Section

| Field | Description | Source |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Home address | Current mailing address | May differ from W-2 Box f |
| City, state, ZIP | Current location | User provides |

## Income Section

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Line 1a: Wages, salaries, tips">
    **Value:** Sum of all W-2 Box 1 amounts
    
    **Source:** W-2 Box 1 (total taxable federal wages)
    
    **Note:** This is your gross wages MINUS pre-tax deductions (401k, HSA, health insurance). It's lower than your actual salary if you have pre-tax benefits.
    
    **Example:**
    ```
    W-2 #1 Box 1: $44,629
    W-2 #2 Box 1: $12,500
    Line 1a: $57,129
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 1z: Total wages">
    **Value:** Same as Line 1a
    
    **Calculation:** Line 1a + Lines 1b through 1h (if applicable)
    
    **Note:** For single W-2 filers with no dependents, only Line 1a applies.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 8: Other income">
    **Value:** From Schedule 1, Line 10
    
    **Source:** Unemployment income, gambling winnings, prizes, jury duty pay
    
    **Note:** Usually $0 for typical W-2 filers
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 9: Total income">
    **Value:** Line 1z + Line 8
    
    **Calculation:**
    ```
    Line 9 = Line 1a + Line 8
    ```
    
    This is your total income before adjustments.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Adjustments to Income

| Line | Field Name | Value | Source |
|------|------------|-------|--------|
| **10** | Adjustments to income | Schedule 1, Line 26 | Student loan interest, educator expenses, etc. |
| **11** | Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) | Line 9 - Line 10 | **This is your AGI** |

<Note>
  Your **AGI** (Line 11) is critical—it's used for credit eligibility, phaseouts, and state returns.
</Note>

---

## Deductions Section

| Line | Field Name | Value | Source |
|------|------------|-------|--------|
| **12a** | Standard deduction | $15,750 (under 65)<br/>$17,750 (65+) | Age-based standard deduction |
| **12b** | Charitable deduction | $0 | Only if itemizing (rare for this profile) |
| **12c** | Schedule 1-A total | From Schedule 1-A | Tips, overtime, car loan, senior deduction |
| **13** | Qualified business income | $0 | W-2 filers have no QBI |
| **14** | Total deductions | 12a + 12b + 12c + 13 | Sum of all deductions |
| **15** | Taxable income | Line 11 - Line 14 | Cannot be negative (use $0) |

<Tip>
  **Line 15 (Taxable income)** is the number that determines your tax bracket.
</Tip>

## Tax Calculation

### Line 16: Federal Income Tax

**Source:** Apply tax brackets to Line 15 (taxable income)

**2025 Tax Brackets (Single):**

| Taxable Income Range | Rate | Tax Calculation |
|---------------------|------|------------------|
| $0 - $11,925 | 10% | taxable_income × 0.10 |
| $11,926 - $48,475 | 12% | $1,192.50 + (taxable_income - $11,925) × 0.12 |
| $48,476 - $103,350 | 22% | $5,578.50 + (taxable_income - $48,475) × 0.22 |
| $103,351 - $197,300 | 24% | $17,651.00 + (taxable_income - $103,350) × 0.24 |
| $197,301 - $250,525 | 32% | $40,099.00 + (taxable_income - $197,300) × 0.32 |
| $250,526 - $626,350 | 35% | $57,131.00 + (taxable_income - $250,525) × 0.35 |
| Over $626,350 | 37% | $188,769.75 + (taxable_income - $626,350) × 0.37 |

**Example:**
```
Taxable income (Line 15): $35,000
Bracket: $11,926 - $48,475 (12%)
Tax = $1,192.50 + ($35,000 - $11,925) × 0.12
Tax = $1,192.50 + $2,769.00
Line 16: $3,961.50
```

### Additional Tax Lines

| Line | Field Name | Value | Source |
|------|------------|-------|--------|
| **17** | Schedule 2 amount | $0 | AMT or excess premium tax credit (rare) |
| **18** | Total tax before credits | Line 16 + Line 17 | Usually same as Line 16 |
| **19** | Nonrefundable credits | From Schedule 3 | Saver's Credit if eligible |
| **22** | Tax after credits | Line 18 - Line 19 | Cannot go below $0 |
| **23** | Other taxes | $0 | Schedule 2 additional taxes |
| **24** | **Total tax** | Line 22 + Line 23 | **Your final tax liability** |

## Payments Section

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Line 25a: Federal tax withheld from W-2s">
    **Value:** Sum of all W-2 Box 2 amounts
    
    **Source:** W-2 Box 2 (federal income tax withheld)
    
    **Example:**
    ```
    W-2 #1 Box 2: $4,200
    W-2 #2 Box 2: $1,100
    Line 25a: $5,300
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 25d: Total federal tax withheld">
    **Value:** Line 25a + Line 25b + Line 25c
    
    **Note:** For W-2-only filers, this equals Line 25a
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 27a: Earned Income Credit (EIC)">
    **Eligibility:** Single filers with AGI ≤ $19,104 (no children)
    
    **Value:** Based on earned income and AGI
    
    **Note:** Must be between 25 and 64 years old
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Line 33: Total payments">
    **Value:** Line 25d + Line 27a + other credits
    
    **This is the total you've already paid** (withholding + refundable credits)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refund or Amount Owed

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Refund (Line 34)" icon="circle-check" color="#16a34a">
    **If Line 33 > Line 24:**
    
    Refund = Line 33 - Line 24
    
    **Direct deposit info:**

    - Line 35c: Account type (Checking/Savings)
    - Line 35d: Account number
  </Card>

    Line 38: Estimated tax penalty (if underpaid)

## Supporting Schedules

### Schedule 1: Additional Income and Adjustments

**When needed:** Student loan interest deduction, educator expenses, unemployment income

| Line | Field | Value | Source |
|------|-------|-------|--------|
| 21 | Student loan interest | min($2,500, interest paid) | 1098-E form, subject to phaseout |
| 26 | Total adjustments | Sum of Part II | Flows to Form 1040 Line 10 |

**Phaseout:** $85,000 - $100,000 MAGI

### Schedule 1-A: New Deductions (2025)

**When needed:** Tips, overtime pay, car loan interest, senior deduction

| Line | Field | Max Amount | Phaseout |
|------|-------|------------|----------|
| 1 | Tip income deduction | Actual tips | $75K - $100K MAGI |
| 2 | Overtime pay deduction | Actual overtime | $75K - $100K MAGI |
| 3 | Auto loan interest | $10,000 | $75K - $100K MAGI |
| 4 | Senior deduction (65+) | $4,000 | $75K - $100K MAGI |
| 5 | Total | Sum of Lines 1-4 | Flows to Form 1040 Line 12c |

### Form 8880: Saver's Credit

**When needed:** AGI ≤ $39,500 AND retirement contributions exist

| Line | Field | Value |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Retirement contributions | W-2 Box 12 (codes D, E, G, AA, BB) |
| 2 | Maximum eligible | $2,000 |
| 3 | Eligible amount | min(Line 1, $2,000) |
| 4 | Credit rate | 50%, 20%, or 10% (based on AGI) |
| 5 | Credit amount | Line 3 × Line 4 |

**AGI Thresholds (Single):**
- 50% credit: AGI ≤ $23,750
- 20% credit: $23,751 - $25,850
- 10% credit: $25,851 - $39,500

**Flow:** Form 8880 → Schedule 3 Line 4 → Form 1040 Line 19

## Key Formulas

```
AGI (Line 11) = Total Income (Line 9) - Adjustments (Line 10)

Taxable Income (Line 15) = AGI - Total Deductions (Line 14)

Total Tax (Line 24) = Tax from Brackets (Line 16) - Credits (Line 19)

Refund (Line 34) = Payments (Line 33) - Total Tax (Line 24)  [if positive]

Amount Owed (Line 37) = Total Tax (Line 24) - Payments (Line 33)  [if positive]
```

<Warning>
  All line numbers reference the **2025 Form 1040**. Line numbers change occasionally—always verify against the current year's form.
</Warning>
